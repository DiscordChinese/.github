## 🌟 关于我们 | About Us

**Discord Chinese** 是一个完全由志愿者、中文母语者以及全球中文爱好者共同驱动的非盈利社区。我们不仅提供一个练习中文口语、听力和写作的平台，更致力于搭建一座连接中国传统文化、现代流行趋势（如游戏、网络热梗）与世界各地朋友的桥梁。

*Discord Chinese is a non-profit community fully driven by volunteers, native Chinese speakers, and global language enthusiasts. We offer a platform to practice spoken Chinese, listening, and writing, while building a bridge that connects traditional Chinese culture, modern pop trends (like gaming and internet slang), and friends from all over the world.*

> 💡 **特别声明 (Note):** 本 GitHub 组织下的部分代码、文案及项目架构由 AI 辅助生成与优化，旨在提升社区运营效率与开源透明度。<br>
> *Some code, copy, and project architecture within this GitHub organization are assisted and optimized by AI, aiming to improve community operational efficiency and open-source transparency.*

## 🤝 参与贡献 | Get Involved

无论你是**前端开发者、Python 工程师**，还是**中文教师、设计爱好者**，我们都非常欢迎你参与到 Discord Chinese 的建设中来！

*Whether you are a **frontend developer**, **Python engineer**, **Chinese teacher**, or **design enthusiast**, you are highly welcome to contribute to Discord Chinese!*


1. **提交 PR (Pull Requests)**：欢迎对我们的官网代码提出优化，或是修复错别字。
    - Submit a PR: Feel free to optimize our website code or fix any typos.
3. **提出 Issue (Issues)**：如果你在社区网站或工具中发现 Bug，或者有很棒的新功能建议。
    - Open an Issue: Report bugs found in our website/tools, or suggest exciting new features.
3. **加入 Discord**：最好的贡献方式就是直接加入我们的服务器，与其他成员用中文交流！
    - Join Discord: The best way to contribute is by joining our server and chatting with members in Chinese!
